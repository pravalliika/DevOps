What is DevOps?

DevOps is a process of improving a continous delivery by ensuring that there is a proper automation and quality in place 
and ensuring that it have proper continous monitoring and continous testing.

Why DevOps?

To fulfill the need of delivering more and faster and better application to meet more and more demands of users ,we need DevOps.
DevOps helps deployment to happen really fast compared to any other traditional tools.

The key aspects or principle behind DevOps is:
• Infrastructure as a Code
• Continuous Integration
• Continuous Deployment
• Automation
• Continuous Monitoring
• Security
